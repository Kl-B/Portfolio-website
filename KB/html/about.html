About Me


I am originally from the countryside of the Swedish West Coast. After a few adventures in other towns in Sweden and a short time in Paris, France I’m back on the West Coast living with my three cats. Animals and nature are things that I find important and it’s also how I clear my head and recharge. My upbringing has made me a responsible, thorough and caring person with a high work ethic.

At work I enjoy roles that are versatile with room for creativity. I thrive in teams with good communication and common goals. When offered a job at Kontorsleverantören I accepted the challenge with the believe that it would last for one summer. The company itself and my wonderful colleagues made me come back however and I have found a great appreciation for loyalty at my place of work. My time with them taught me all I know about communication, cooperation and that I can contribute to a fun dynamic at my place of work.
